Chicken Shawarama

Chicken Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made of marinated chicken that is slow-roasted on a vertical rotisserie. It is typically seasoned with a blend of spices like cumin, paprika, garlic, and turmeric, then sliced thin and served in pita bread or flatbread with toppings like garlic sauce, tahini, pickles, and vegetables.

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Pita bread
  • Coriander
  • Cayenne
  • Garlic powder

Peperation

  1. Marinate the Chicken
    • In a bowl, mix yogurt, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and all the spices. Coat the chicken with the marinade and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best flavor).
  2. Cook the Chicken
    • Grill Method: Heat a grill pan or outdoor grill and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side until charred and fully cooked.
    • Stovetop Method: Heat a pan with a little oil, cook the chicken on medium-high heat for 5-6 minutes per side until golden brown.
    • Oven Method: Preheat to 220°C (425°F), place chicken on a baking tray, and bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway.
  3. Slice the Chicken
    • Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into thin strips.
  4. Prepare the Garlic Sauce
    • Mix mayonnaise/yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and salt. Adjust to taste.
  5. Assemble the Shawarma Wrap
    • Warm the pita bread.
    • Spread garlic sauce.
    • Add sliced chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, and pickles.
    • Wrap it tightly and enjoy!
