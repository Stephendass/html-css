Chicken Shawarama

Chicken Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made of marinated chicken that is slow-roasted on a vertical rotisserie. It is typically seasoned with a blend of spices like cumin, paprika, garlic, and turmeric, then sliced thin and served in pita bread or flatbread with toppings like garlic sauce, tahini, pickles, and vegetables.

Total time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Pita bread
  • Coriander
  • Cayenne
  • Garlic powder
